Intro To VEX Robotics For New Mexico

About

Hey guys! This is Grant Staten. If you competed at all in VRC in New Mexico from Tower Takeover through Spin Up then you probably already at least know of me. But in case you don't, I'm currently a senior at La Cueva High School and have been a part of La Cueva's robotics program since it started at the beginning of my Freshman year. I was heavily involved in the club my freshman year and have been running La Cueva's robotics program for the last 3 years since then. In VEX robotics there is a huge issue for new teams of just not having available information. I see a lot of teams in New Mexico that have dedicated members and the necessary resources to compete, but are unable to compete at their highest potential level because they don't have information, and these videos aim to help solve that and give never teams resources to help get them started.

Also I know a quite a bit about VEX robotics, but not much about making good videos, they aren't going to be nicely edited or anything, may be slow or fast, and honestly a lot of them ended up just being me rambling about the video's topic by the end. So feel free to put me on 1.5x speed or pause and play or whatever you want as you go through these.
